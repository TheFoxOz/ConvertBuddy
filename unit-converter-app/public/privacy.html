Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2024

1. Introduction

ConvertBuddy ("we," "our," or "us") is a free unit and currency converter tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect.

No Personal Information Collected: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information about you.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our service:

  • Anonymous Device ID: A randomly generated ID stored locally in your browser to save your conversion history (optional feature).
  • Conversion History: Your conversion calculations are stored anonymously in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud) only if you use the history feature. This data is linked only to your anonymous device ID.
  • Currency Exchange Rates: Cached from a public API (ExchangeRate-API) to enable offline currency conversions. No personal data is involved.
  • Cookies and Local Storage: We use browser local storage to save your device ID and conversion preferences. We also use cookies via Google AdSense (see Section 4 below).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide unit and currency conversion functionality
  • To save your conversion history across sessions (optional)
  • To cache currency rates for offline use
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we store about you (anonymous device ID and conversion history).
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your conversion history. You can also clear it manually using the "Clear All" button in the app, or by clearing your browser's local storage.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of personalized advertising via Google Ad Settings.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data for advertising purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at convertbuddy@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored anonymously (no personal identifiers)
  • Firebase data is secured with Google's infrastructure and encryption
  • Local storage is only accessible by your browser
  • We use HTTPS to encrypt data in transit

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Conversion History: Stored indefinitely unless you clear it manually or request deletion.
  • Cookies: AdSense cookies expire according to Google's retention policy (typically 90 days to 2 years).
  • Currency Rates Cache: Refreshed every 24 hours.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country, including in the United States (Google Firebase, AdSense). By using our service, you consent to these transfers.
