Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

1. Introduction

ConvertBuddy is a free offline-first unit and currency converter. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Anonymous Usage ID: A random ID stored locally to save your conversion history.
  • Conversion History: Only stored if you allow it (optional, anonymous).
  • Currency Rates: Cached from a public API (no personal data).

3. No Personal Data

We do not collect names, emails, location, IP addresses, or any identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase only for anonymous history storage (optional) and Google AdSense for non-intrusive ads.

5. Contact

For any questions, contact us at: convertbuddy@gmail.com
