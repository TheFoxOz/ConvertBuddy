               Privacy Policy            

           

Last updated: November 26, 2025

       
         
           

1. Introduction

           

               Welcome to ConvertBuddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.            

           

                This app is primarily a functional utility and does not aim to collect extensive personal data.            

       
         
           

2. Data We Collect

           
                   
  •                    Conversion History:                     We store your conversion inputs (value, units, category) and results in our database (Firestore) to provide a persistent "Conversion History" feature. This data is associated with a unique user ID.                
    •                
  •                    Unique User ID:                    When you use ConvertBuddy, we automatically assign you a unique ID via Firebase Authentication (either an anonymous ID or a temporary custom ID). This ID is used exclusively to link your conversion history to your session. We do not link this ID to your real-world identity or external accounts.                
    •                
  •                    Usage Data:                     We may collect non-identifiable usage data to monitor the performance of the app, such as which unit categories are most popular.                
    •            
       
         
           

3. How We Use Your Information

           

                We use the information we collect to:            

           
                   
  •                    Provide the Service:                     To perform unit and currency conversions and display your saved history.                
    •                
  •                    Maintain Security:                     To ensure the database functions correctly and securely.                
    •                
  •                    Improve the App:                     To understand usage patterns and enhance app features.                
    •            
       
         
           

4. Data Storage and Security

           

                Your data, primarily conversion history, is stored securely using Google's Firebase Firestore service. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.            

       
         
           

5. Your Privacy Rights

           

                You have the right to request access to the data we have stored for your unique user ID and the right to request that it be deleted. Since your user ID is only displayed in the application, please provide your current user ID (visible in the History section of the app) in any data request.            

       
         
           

6. Contact Us

           

                If you have questions or comments about this policy, please contact us through the platform where you are using this application.
